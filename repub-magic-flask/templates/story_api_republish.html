For Other Publications

Side Effects Public Media welcomes the republication of any of our original reporting. However, we do ask that you do the following:

  1. If you’re republishing online, include our tracking tag, which tells us who is sharing our stories. To do this, following the instructions included on ths page.
  2. We do not permit use of Side Effects content without our permission, or use of our content online without the tracking tag. If you wish to republish in print, or cannot use our tracking tag for some reason, e-mail our editor at healthnews@wfyi.org
  3. Credit and link back to us as follows: This story was produced by Side Effects Public Media, a news collaborative covering public health.
  4. Don’t edit our material, except to reflect relative changes in time or location (For example, "yesterday" can be changed to "last week," and "Columbia, Missouri" to "Columbia" or "here.") or for editorial style.
  5. Include all links from our story.
  6. If you republish our photographs, use the same credits that we use.
  7. Use our reporters’ bylines. We prefer “Author Name, Side Effects.”
